Background

Teamwork Transport Solutions LLP. We came to existence on 11 Oct 2019 in the heart of holy land of Ayodhya. We started operation our first service on 15th January 2020, from Jalalpur ,Ambedkar Nagar to Medical College, Lucknow. Over the period we started facilitating almost all type of services associated with transport domain. Although some of them are not functional but will start them soon. In form of Metroline we entered in bus service segment. After this in form of Metrocab we started operating cab operation. We also planned a trip planning devision as Tripbuzz.In near future we have planned to enter in resort business, holiday planner & cargo operations.
